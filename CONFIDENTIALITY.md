# Confidentiality & Data Privacy Policy

## Our Committment to Privacy
CommunityConnect AI respects the confidentiality and privacy of all our users, including volunteers, NGOs, and community beneficiaries. As an AI-powered platform handling potentially sensitive social welfare data, we adhere to strict data protection standards.

## Data Collection & Usage
1. **Minimal Data Collection:** We collect only the data reasonably necessary to connect volunteers with relevant social causes and missions.
2. **AI Modeling:** Our matching algorithms process data solely to optimize volunteer deployments. Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is anonymized before being used in any macro-level ML model training.
3. **No Third-Party Selling:** User data, including location information, skill profiles, and contribution histories, will **never** be sold to third parties or used for external marketing purposes.

## Security Controls
- **Encryption:** All data in transit and at rest is securely encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
- **Authentication:** Strict role-based access control (RBAC) ensures only authorized personnel or NGO leads can view beneficiary requirements.
- **Blockchain Verification:** Volunteer impact hours are tokenized (NFT) securely on the Polygon network without exposing underlying identity details.

## Transparency & User Rights
Users retain full ownership of their data and can:
- Request a full copy of their collected data.
- Opt out of AI data processing features.
- Request immediate deletion of their account and all associated personal history.

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@communityconnect.ai.
